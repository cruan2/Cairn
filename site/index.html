Cairn

Your higher-elo friend, 30 seconds before the game.

Cairn is a free, non-commercial coaching tool for League of Legends.

What it is

Given the two team compositions, Cairn writes a short, memorable pre-game note — your win condition, the enemy's win condition, lane-matchup advice, key power spikes, and the mistakes to avoid — in the voice of a higher-ranked player briefing a friend before a game.

It's built for players who know the basics but lack the pattern recognition of an experienced player: the goal is intuition and mental models, not "farm more."

How it works

  • A curated coaching engine turns champion identities and matchups into the note.
  • To keep advice grounded in reality, Cairn aggregates lane-phase statistics (gold and CS differentials, first-objective and plate timings) from public ranked matches, broken down by rank and patch.
  • An optional companion reads your own live game — via Riot's local Live Client Data API on 127.0.0.1:2999 — to show the relevant note automatically.

How it uses the Riot API

account-v4 / summoner-v4
Resolve a player's Riot ID to fetch their own match history and verify account ownership.
league-v4
Read ranked tier, to label aggregated stats by elo and sample high-elo games.
match-v5
Read match and timeline data to compute the lane-phase statistics that ground the advice.
lol-status-v4
Service status checks.

Data & privacy

  • Non-commercial personal project, used by the developer and a small group of friends.
  • Respects Riot API rate limits; does not scrape Riot or third-party sites.
  • Stores only derived aggregate statistics — not a bulk copy of raw match data.
  • Account ownership is verified with Riot's standard third-party verification code.
